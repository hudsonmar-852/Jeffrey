# Jeffrey Wellness OS — Brand Bible

## 1. Brand role

Jeffrey is not positioned as a hard-selling personal trainer. The brand role is:

> A trusted wellness coach who helps people feel better through small, practical, evidence-informed actions.

## 2. Audience

Primary audiences:

- Previous clients who have not attended classes recently
- Busy Hong Kong office workers
- Adults who feel tired, stiff, inactive, or unsure how to restart
- People who value private, low-pressure support

## 3. Brand promise

**One minute. Better every day.**

The user should always feel that taking one small action is worthwhile.

## 4. Brand values

1. Human first
2. Practical
3. Evidence-informed
4. Warm but professional
5. Non-judgmental
6. Privacy-respecting
7. Simple to act on
8. No pressure to purchase

## 5. Tone of voice

Use:

- Natural Hong Kong Cantonese
- Short, caring sentences
- Practical actions
- Calm encouragement
- Honest limits
- Private support invitation

Avoid:

- Fear-based health claims
- Shame or guilt
- Guaranteed outcomes
- Medical diagnosis
- Aggressive sales language
- Artificial urgency
- Misleading gift value
- Forced forwarding

## 6. Core message structure

1. Relevant opening: daily life, weather, routine, or common concern
2. Genuine care: acknowledge how the person may feel
3. One action: 30–90 seconds
4. Small insight: why it may help
5. Optional challenge: simple completion
6. Surprise unlock: useful, truthful, and proportionate
7. Ripple invitation: invite one person they care about
8. Private message: low-pressure support from Jeffrey

## 7. Preferred language

Preferred:

- 「今日畀自己一分鐘」
- 「唔需要勉強自己」
- 「完成一個小行動已經好好」
- 「如果想私下傾兩句，Jeffrey 一直都喺度」
- 「邀請一位你關心嘅人一齊完成」

Avoid:

- 「立即購買」
- 「最後機會」
- 「保證改善」
- 「你再唔做就會……」
- 「轉發俾十個人」

## 8. Reward principles

Rewards must be real, clearly described, and never exaggerated.

Recommended reward types:

- Recovery insight
- Short guided reset
- Members-only protocol
- Wellness checklist
- Personal reflection prompt
- Limited participation reward
- Transparent lucky draw entry

Do not invent retail values or imply everyone receives a physical product unless that is true.

## 9. Private message principle

The CTA is not “Book now” or “Contact trainer”. Use:

> 私下同 Jeffrey 傾兩句

It should be optional, respectful, and clear that users do not need to buy a class.

## 10. Health and safety standard

All content is general education, not diagnosis or treatment. Every protocol must include appropriate stop conditions and recommend professional medical help for severe, persistent, worsening, traumatic, neurological, or unexplained symptoms.
