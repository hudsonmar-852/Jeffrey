# AI Content Rules

## Content objective

Generate one concise, useful wellness interaction that feels like genuine care from Jeffrey rather than marketing.

## Required output sequence

1. Contextual opening
2. Empathetic observation
3. One safe, simple action
4. Brief explanation
5. Optional challenge
6. Truthful surprise unlock
7. Care-based ripple invitation
8. Optional private message CTA

## Source policy

- Weather, current news, public alerts, product details, research updates, laws, and schedules must be verified from current reliable sources.
- Prefer official or primary sources for health, weather, and research.
- Do not turn unrelated tragedy, politics, accidents, or distressing news into promotional content.
- Reject a current-news hook when the connection to wellness is forced.

## Medical safety gate

AI must not:

- Diagnose a condition
- Promise treatment or recovery
- Claim a movement is suitable for everyone
- Advise ignoring pain
- Replace medical care
- Present anecdote as proof

Each protocol must define:

- Intended audience
- Duration and intensity
- Simple steps
- Common mistakes
- Stop conditions
- When to seek qualified medical advice

Stop immediately and recommend professional assessment where appropriate for severe pain, worsening pain, recent trauma, chest pain, breathing difficulty, fainting, unexplained weakness, numbness, loss of coordination, or other concerning symptoms.

## Message quality rules

- Traditional Chinese, natural Hong Kong usage
- Prefer 80–160 Chinese characters for outbound WhatsApp messages
- One main action only
- Avoid repetitive hydration reminders unless contextually relevant
- No fabricated statistics or testimonials
- No fabricated reward value
- No pressure to attend a class
- No requirement to forward

## Reward truthfulness

Allowed:

- A named digital protocol that really exists
- A useful health insight with appropriate caveats
- An entry into a clearly explained lucky draw
- A limited reward with accurate eligibility

Disallowed:

- “Worth HK$300” without a real item and supportable value
- Suggesting every participant receives a physical product when only selected winners do
- Fake countdowns or artificial scarcity
- Hidden data collection as part of a reward

## Ripple copy standard

Use:

> 如果你覺得今日呢一分鐘有幫助，可以邀請一位你關心嘅朋友一齊完成。

Never use chain-message threats, social pressure, or claims that the receiver will be notified for failing to forward.

## Private message standard

Use a low-pressure CTA:

> 最近有邊度繃緊、容易攰，或者只係想分享近況，都可以私下同 Jeffrey 傾兩句。

Contact details are collected only after explicit opt-in and a clear privacy notice.
